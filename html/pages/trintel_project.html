About the Project
Task

The Task of the this project was to construct a working software for the RBZ Kiel. The customer wanted a software to support their stundents in their Business Simulation by tracking their Transactions with their fantasy Bussinesses.

Functionality

Our Software provides the core functions such as User Roles with diffrent Interfaces ass well as the Companies and their transactions. A user is then able to make transactions with their Company and gets feddback from the Software in form of Statistiks, Sales History or Confirmation of order. Furthermore the Admin has possibiltys of adjusting the whole Simulation.
