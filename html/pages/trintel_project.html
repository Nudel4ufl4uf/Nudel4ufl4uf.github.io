About the Project
Task

The task of this project consisted in building a website for the RBZ Kiel. The customer wanted a software to support their students in their Business Simulation by monitoring their transactions with their imaginary business. We had two weeks to complete this task and present it to the RBZ.

Functionality

Our software provides the basic features like user roles with different interfaces, as well as the Companies and their transactions. A user is then able to perform transactions with their company and get feedback from the software in the form of statistics, sales history or order confirmation. In addition, it is possible for the administrator to adjust the entire simulation.
