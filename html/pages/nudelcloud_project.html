About the Project
Task

With this little project, I have deployed my own cloud, based on Nextcloud.

Functionality

This Cloud-Storage is selfhosted at a german server provider to get the best of both sides being performance and privacy. Due to the location being in the EU the data protection law is a lot better than all the other providers such as Google or Microsoft wich are hosting in the USA. Furthermore Nextcloud is open-source and offers a lot of extra features such as an email client, office tools, calender and a lot more. I am Using it as my Hub for everything important during my daily routine. For more information visit:

https://nextcloud.com/athome/
