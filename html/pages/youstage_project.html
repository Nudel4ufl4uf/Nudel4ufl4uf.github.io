About the Project
Task

The YouStage application is a prototype based upon a student project from early 2020. In the planning phase, the idea was to become familiar with the prototype process. Once the module was completed, I dived deeper by doing a front-end-only application with flutter to find out more about flutter and git.

Functionality

The project was intended to serve as a platform for a variety of artists who are unable to perform as normal due to COVID-19. It is supposedly a platform like Twitch, but with more interactive features for the artist in particular. Most of the requirements may be seen in the "Anforderungsanalysis" in you stadium/ Prototype. After the project crashed due to the end of the semester in the middle of 2020, it was my idea to keep it alive in the form of a small prototype. The full Prototype can be seen under youstage/Prototype.
