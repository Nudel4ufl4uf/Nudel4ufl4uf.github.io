About the Project
Task

The Idea was to deploy Mycroft as a privacy First Home Assistant solution on my raspberry pi.

Functionality

I turned my raspberry pi into an Home Assistant replycing my Google home due to privacy reasons. After connecting my external mic and speaker as well as installing the Mycroft distrobution, I then had to go through all the configration steps. Due to the big ammount of settings I was able to personalize Mycroft to my personal needs.
